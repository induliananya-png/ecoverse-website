Welcome to EcoVerse

EcoVerse is a platform built to raise awareness on importance of protecting life on land. The site highlights the urgent need to conserve terrestrial ecosystems, save endangered species and safeguard biodiversty for present and future generations. Based on SDG 15, we aim to inform, inspire and involve you in protecting Earth's precious biodiversity.

Learning about life on land is the first step to protecting it. Inside EcoVerse, you will discover interactive facts, photo galleries, feedback opportunities and personal insights from each team member which are created to strengthen the connection with the natural world.
